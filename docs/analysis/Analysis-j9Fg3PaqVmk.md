## Context & Key Takeaways
This video introduces a higher level of technical precision by combining **SMC (Smart Money Concepts)** with specific **Candlestick Patterns**. The key insight is that a candle's meaning depends on its *location* and its *role* (e.g., a "Liquidity Sweep" vs. "Structure Shift").

1. **Entry Confirmation**: Don't enter just because price is in a zone; wait for the *candle* to confirm the move.
2. **"Smart Money" zones**: Focus only on candles closing in specific locations like Order Blocks (OB) or Breaker Blocks.
3. **Execution Timing**: Fine-tuning entry points to reduce "Stop Loss" size and increase R:R.
