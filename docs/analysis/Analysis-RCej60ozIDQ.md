## Context & Key Takeaways
This video serves as a comprehensive foundational training for traders moving into 2026. The primary shift is the transition from "Retail" indicators to "Institutional" logic, also known as **Smart Money Concepts (SMC)**.

### Key Takeaways
1. **The retail trap**: Traditional indicators (RSI, EMA, MACD) are often used as "liquidity" by institutions.
2. **Liquidity is king**: Markets move to hunt for liquidity (Stop Losses) to fill large institutional orders.
3. **Psychology of 2026**: Modern markets are faster and more algo-driven; understanding the underlying institutional intent is the only way to stay profitable.

## Main Points
- **Market Structure**: Identifying the true trend versus sub-structures.
- **Liquidity Pools**: Areas where large clusters of orders sit (equal highs/lows).
- **Institutional Order Flow**: Understanding where "Smart Money" is entering the market.
- **Psychology**: Moving away from emotional trading to a rule-based SMC approach.

## Good Things (Pros)

## Things to NOT do
- **Don't use lagging indicators**: Avoid relying on EMA crossovers or RSI overbought/oversold levels.
- **Don't ignore the multi-timeframe structure**: A good pattern on M5 can be a trap if it's against the H1/H4 structure.
- **Don't trade without a "Liquidity" reason**: If you don't know where the liquidity is, *you* are the liquidity.
